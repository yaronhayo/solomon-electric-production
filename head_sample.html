Stopping Domestic Electrical Hazards in Plantation

Electrical issues in Plantation are often exacerbated by Broward County's high humidity and salt-air environment. Ignoring these warning signs can lead to property damage or safety hazards.

No Neutral Wire in Switch Box

Many smart switches require a neutral wire that older homes lack. We can install smart switches designed for no-neutral situations or run neutral wires where needed.

Multi-Way Switch Confusion

Three-way and four-way switches controlling lights from multiple locations require specific smart switch configurations. DIY mistakes here cause non-functioning switches.

WiFi Dead Zones

Smart devices need reliable WiFi. We can identify connectivity issues and recommend solutions like mesh networks or Zigbee/Z-Wave alternatives.

Dimmer Compatibility Issues

Not all smart dimmers work with all LED bulbs—causing flickering, buzzing, or limited dimming range. We select compatible equipment combinations.

App and Voice Setup Complexity

Getting smart devices to work together across apps and voice assistants can be frustrating. We configure everything so it works on installation day.

Want Automation Without Replacing Every Switch

Strategic smart switch placement at key locations provides most automation benefits without the cost of replacing every switch in your home.
