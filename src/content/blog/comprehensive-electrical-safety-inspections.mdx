Comprehensive Electrical Safety Inspections

Safeguard your property with detailed electrical system evaluations. Our certified inspectors identify safety risks, outdated wiring, capacity issues, and code violations, providing prioritized recommendations for correction.

## Electrical Inspections
Regular inspections prevent costly hazards. We assess everything from panels to outlets, ensuring all systems meet NEC and local standards.
Our reports highlight actionable fixes and long-term improvements, giving you confidence your property is safe and efficient.

### What is included in your electrical safety inspection?
We conduct a full evaluation of your electrical system, including wiring, outlets, switches, breakers, distribution panels, grounding, load capacity, and potential fire-risk points.

*   **Advanced Diagnostics:** We use professional-grade diagnostic tools including thermal imaging, load testers, multimeters, outlet analyzers, and moisture sensors to detect faults quickly and accurately.
*   **Thorough Reporting:** You will receive a detailed report outlining our findings and recommendations.
