Outlet & Switch Modernization Services

Upgrade your home or office with GFCI/AFCI outlets, smart switches, dimmers, and USB ports. We deliver both style and safety with NEC-compliant modern electrical access.

## Outlet & Switch Installation
We replace outdated outlets and switches with high-quality, modern alternatives that enhance convenience and reduce risk.
Enjoy safer power access and updated aesthetics with expert installation from Solomon Electric.

### What does outlet and switch modernization include?
It includes replacing old outlets and switches with new, code-compliant, stylish, and energy-efficient models. We upgrade wiring connections, install modern safety features, and ensure smooth, reliable performance.

### Upgrade Options
*   **Safety First:** We install GFCI outlets in kitchens, bathrooms, garages, and outdoors for shock protection, and AFCI breakers/outlets to prevent arc-fault fire risks.
*   **Smart Home Ready:** Upgrade to smart switches, dimmers, and USB-integrated outlets for modern convenience.
*   **Aesthetic Upgrade:** Match your outlets and switches to your interior design with a wide range of styles and finishes.
