Lighting Fixture Installation

Professional lighting installations that improve comfort, safety, and visual appeal. We specialize in both residential and commercial setups, from indoor upgrades to outdoor enhancements.

## Lighting Installation
We install, replace, and test fixtures of all kinds, ensuring proper mounting, wiring, and even brightness.
Trust Solomon Electric for lighting that’s as functional as it is beautiful.

### What types of lighting fixtures do you install?
We install ceiling lights, pendant lights, chandeliers, recessed lighting, wall sconces, vanity lights, outdoor lights, LED panels, and smart lighting fixtures—both residential and commercial.

### Our Approach
*   **Design Consultation:** We help you choose the right fixtures to match your style and needs.
*   **Precision Installation:** Securing fixtures safely, especially heavy chandeliers or high-ceiling fans.
*   **LED Upgrades:** Transitioning to energy-efficient LED lighting to save on utility bills.
