## Common Panel Upgrade Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Installing an EV Charger

**Challenge**: Your 100amp panel doesn't have capacity for a Level 2 EV charger (requires 40-50 amps).

**Solution**: Upgrade to 200-amp service provides capacity for EV charger plus all existing loads.

**Related**: [Level 2 EV Charger Installation](/services/level-2-ev-charger-installation) | [Tesla & Universal EV Charging Guide](/blog/tesla-universal-ev-charging-station-installation)

### Scenario 2: Federal Pacific Panel

**Challenge**: Your panel is labeled "FPE" or "Federal Pacific"â€”a documented fire hazard.

**Solution**: Immediate replacement regardless of age. These panels have breakers that fail to trip.

**Related**: [Panel Modernization Guide](/blog/electrical-panel-modernization-upgrades)

### Scenario 3: Frequently Tripping Breakers

**Challenge**: Breakers trip when running AC and other appliances simultaneously.

**Solution**: Load calculation determines if you need more capacity (panel upgrade) or circuit redistribution (panel repair).

**Related**: [Panel Repair Guide](/blog/electrical-panel-repair) | [Electrical Diagnostics](/blog/expert-electrical-diagnostics-repair)

### Scenario 4: Home Renovation/Addition

**Challenge**: Adding square footage, kitchen upgrade, or pool installation requires more electrical capacity.

**Solution**: Load assessment determines if existing panel can support additions or needs upgrade.

**Related**: [Comprehensive Installation Services](/blog/comprehensive-electrical-installation-services)

## The Panel Upgrade Process in Miami

### Step 1: Assessment & Load Calculation
Professional electrician evaluates current system and calculates total electrical load including future needs.

### Step 2: Permit Application
Submit plans to Miami-Dade or Broward building department. We handle all permit paperwork.

### Step 3: FPL Coordination
Coordinate service upgrade with Florida Power & Light including meter upgrade if needed.

### Step 4: Installation Day
- Morning: FPL disconnects power
- Midday: Panel installation (4-6 hours)
- Afternoon: Building inspection
- Late afternoon: FPL reconnects service

### Step 5: Final Verification
Test all circuits, label panel clearly, provide documentation.

**Timeline**: Most residential upgrades complete in one day.
