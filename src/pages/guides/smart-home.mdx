## Installation Requirements

### Electrical Needs

**Neutral Wires**: Most smart switches require neutral wires (white wire) at switch box. Older homes may need rewiring.

**WiFi Coverage**: Strong WiFi at switch/device locations essential for reliable control.

**Compatible Bulbs**: Smart switches work with any bulbs; smart bulbs require standard switches or adapters.

**Load Capacity**: Verify smart switches rated for your light fixture wattage.
